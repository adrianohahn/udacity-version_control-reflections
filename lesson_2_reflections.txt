Q: What happens when you initialize a repository? Why do you need to do it?
A: Git creates metainformation about your files and directories. That is necessary because git needs to control the status of your files.

Q: How is the staging area different from the working directory and the repository? What value do you think it offers?
A: Staging area provides a way to select what should be commited. It allows you to commit just the files that are related to a logical change.
